The biggest hurdle for businesses trying to adopt AI design tools is what the industry calls **AI drift**.

## The Problem with Midjourney and DALL-E
When you prompt a generic image model to create a "blue robot mascot," it will give you a stunning image. However, when you prompt it again slightly differently—"that same blue robot drinking coffee"—the engine creates an entirely new robot that happens to also be blue. 

For brand identity, this is unacceptable. Your audience needs point-and-click recognition.

## Solving it with Identity Lock™
Mascot Maker was built from the ground up to solve AI drift. Rather than generating a new character from scratch every time, our engine uses **Identity Lock™**. 

This allows the engine to preserve facial structure, eye spacing, specific hex-color references, and body proportions. You can generate a neutral logo, a happy thumbs-up sticker, and a stressed 404-error graphic, and they will undeniably be the exact same character.
