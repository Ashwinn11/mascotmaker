When it comes to building your brand's character, there are two paths: hiring a professional illustrator or leveraging an AI design studio like Mascot Maker.

## The Cost Equation
Hiring a freelance illustrator for a comprehensive mascot package (which includes the character concept plus 5-10 distinct poses for different use cases) generally starts around $2,000 and can scale to $15,000+ for 3D modeling and rendering.

AI generation, on the other hand, shifts the cost structure to zero or near-zero. With Mascot Maker, you can generate initial concepts and dozens of poses for the price of a monthly subscription (or for free during your trial).

## Turnaround Time
The back-and-forth revision cycle of manual illustration often delays launches by 3 to 6 weeks. Mascot Maker operates in ~30 seconds per pose, allowing you to react to cultural trends and memes on social media instantly.

## When to Use Which?
If you require extreme stylistic constraints (such as integrating your mascot physically into a complex custom UI interacting with abstract elements), a human illustrator remains superior. But if you need high-quality 2D or 3D characters for thumbnails, avatars, sticker packs, and general branding, an AI engine utilizing Identity Lock will save you thousands of dollars while moving at lightspeed.
